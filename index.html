Welcome to my Rock Paper Scissors game!

Game instructions:

Players start each round by choosing either: Rock, Paper or Scissors by clicking the icons on the left-hand side. Your goal is to get as many points as possible before the game ends. When the computer score reaches 5 points the game will automatically end. Good luck.

  • Rock wins against scissors.
  • Paper wins against rock.
  • Scissors win against paper.
